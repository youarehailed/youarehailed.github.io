Game rules: Two players take turns placing black or white pieces on the board. The first player to get 4 pieces on a row, column or diagonal, wins the game! When the board is full, the game is a draw

Experimental procedure: We recruited 30 participants for 5 sessions, spaced no more than 3 days apart. On each session, the participant played against AI opponents for 60 minutes, switching colors after each game. After each game, the AI changed its settings. When the participant won, the AI became stronger, when the participant lost, the AI became weaker. We compensated participants $12 per session plus $12 completion bonus, but gave no performance bonus.
