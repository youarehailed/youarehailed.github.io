Game rules: Two players take turns placing black or white pieces on the board. The first player to get 4 pieces on a row, column or diagonal, wins the game! When the board is full, the game is a draw

Experimental procedure: We recruited 30 participants. Each participant played against AI opponents for 30 minutes and completed 82 trials each of a two-alternative forced-choice task and an evaluation task. On an evaluation trial, the participant sees a board position and indicates their estimate of the winning chances of the active player (denoted above the board) on a 7-point scale. We compensated participants $12 for their time, but gave no performance bonus.
